MONKEY-TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a popular, known for its wooden steps and bamboo handrails, and for the monkeys that can be seen along the way. It's a relatively short walk (15-20 minutes) with some steep sections and can be busy.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Path through the jungle with wooden steps & rickety bridges, popular for monkey spotting & sunsets.
